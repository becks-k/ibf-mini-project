Hello Detective!

The game utilizes OpenAI's GPT-3.5 Turbo for mystery generation and game play.
To play a mystery, an OpenAI Api Key is required.

You can generate interactive mysteries based on your specified theme.

By exploring the crime scene and interrogating suspects, you have to deduce:

  1. Who did it?
  2. What weapon was used to carry out the crime?
  3. What is the character's motive for committing the crime?
